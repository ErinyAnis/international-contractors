Who we are and how our journey has been

In the early nineties of the last century, the idea of establishing our business and entering the labor market officially occurred on November 25, 1992. This was the official beginning of work in servicing projects. We, the brothers, began establishing Mustafa Mahmoud and Partners Company for customs and logistical services. The beginning was by serving infrastructure projects. The basic foundation of the Arab Republic of Egypt.. The starting point was from the first and second lines of the Cairo subway, which is the largest station in this honorable march.

From here, we gained experience in providing services to be honored to serve the companies that implemented these projects. We started with the French Société Générale, then Sogea, with whom we worked in the Abu Qir power station project, then the new Qasr Al-Aini Teaching Hospital. We also served the French company Companion Bernard in the first and second phases. For the second line of the subway, then we headed to complete this wonderful group of companies... Ambergello-Cojevar... this is the Italian alliance that we presented in the Esna Barrage project and the electricity station attached to it...

The scope of work expanded, and at the beginning of the third century and the 2000s, we began serving the German-French alliance BELFINGR-BERGR with the French VINCI, and here another story began with the new Assiut Barrage and the power station attached to it. We are honored to win alone the service of the French VINCI, which took upon itself the implementation of the third line of the Greater Cairo Metro. .. And we are still there serving the country with honor and integrity, and for United Services to be a witness to an era of national renaissance that changes the features of the history of modern Egypt, and for us to be among the close ranks as a barrier to the development of society.
